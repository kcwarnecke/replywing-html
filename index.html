Is Reply Wing affiliated with Yelp?+
No. Reply Wing is an independent third-party service that helps you manage your Yelp reviews. We are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Yelp Inc. Yelp® is a registered trademark of Yelp Inc.
Will customers know the response was written by AI?+
No. Every response is drafted in your restaurant's voice and reviewed by you before it goes live. You can edit anything before posting. Customers see a thoughtful, professional response from you — not a robot.
What if I don't like the AI response?+
You have full control. Edit before posting, skip the review entirely, or write your own from scratch. The AI is a starting point — never the final word.
How long does setup take?+
About 5 minutes. We set up a Google Sheet for your restaurant, connect it to our system, and run a test. The free setup call walks you through everything.
What Yelp reviews does it cover?+
All of them. Every new review on your business page — 1★ to 5★ — gets monitored and responded to. We check every 2 hours, day and night.
Do I need any technical skills?+
None. If you can use Google Sheets, you can use Reply Wing. No software to install, no dashboard to learn.
Can I cancel anytime?+
Yes. No contracts, no cancellation fees, no questions asked.
Do you treat all reviews the same?+
No. We analyze every reviewer before drafting. Elite members, loyal customers, and first-timers each get responses calibrated to them. High-influence reviewers get priority replies within 5 minutes.
What about Google, Facebook, TripAdvisor?+
Yelp is our focus right now and we do it exceptionally well. Google, Facebook, and TripAdvisor are coming soon — when you're ready to expand, we'll be ready too.
