Will customers know the response was written by AI?+
No. Every response is drafted in your business's voice and reviewed by you before it goes live. You can edit anything before posting. Customers see a thoughtful, professional response from you — not a robot.
What if I don't like the AI response?+
You have full control. Edit before posting, skip the review entirely, or write your own from scratch. The AI is a starting point — never the final word.
How long does setup take?+
About 5 minutes. We set up a Google Sheet for your business, connect it to our system, and walk you through adding Reply Wing as a manager on each platform. The free setup call covers everything.

Yes. Once you approve a response in your Google Sheet, Reply Wing posts it directly to the platform on your behalf — no copy-pasting, no manual steps. We're authorized to do this because you add Reply Wing as a manager to your Yelp, Google, Facebook, and TripAdvisor accounts during onboarding. Nothing goes live without your approval first.

All of them, across all four platforms — Yelp, Google, Facebook, and TripAdvisor. Every new review — 1★ to 5★ — is detected instantly and responded to, day and night.
Do I need any technical skills?+
None. If you can use Google Sheets, you can use Reply Wing. No software to install, no dashboard to learn.
Can I cancel anytime?+
Yes. No contracts, no cancellation fees, no questions asked.
